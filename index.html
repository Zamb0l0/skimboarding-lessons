Whether you're wanting to give it a go or a seasoned skimmer looking for the next step forward.

Flat & Wave

We have the ability to teach both flatland and wave skimboarding.

Where We Slide

Hibiscus Coast

Perfect for beginners. Those long, shallow flats at Orewa and Red Beach are world-class for learning your first slide.

Mt Maunganui

The home of NZ summer. We hit the Main Beach or Tay Street depending on the tide for that iconic coastal experience.

Coaching Services

Private Lessons

1-on-1 coaching tailored to your skill level. From first slide to wave riding.

Group Sessions

Perfect for friends or family. Learn the basics together in a fun environment.

Video Analysis

Advanced riders: we film your session and break down your technique.

Grab a Spot

Check the calendar for upcoming sessions. I only open dates with the best tides and conditions. Limit 4 people per day.

Life on the Shore

Support the Dream!

My goal is to grow the NZ skimboarding community. To keep the stoke accessible for everyone, I offer a "pay what you want" model for my coaching. Your support helps me keep this idea running and gets more boards under feet.
