Learn to Skimboard

Why Skimboard?

Skimboarding is hard. You fall often, but you keep going. And when it clicks, it’s incredibly rewarding. This is a space to share what we know, what we've learned, and to open the door for others to enjoy the challenge and the ride. If you're curious or already hooked — this is for you.

Beginner Lessons

Start from scratch – no board or experience needed.

Please bring swimwear or a wetsuit. Times are updated weekly and may change depending on weather and tide. Bookings may be rescheduled if needed.

Intermediate Progression

Already sliding? Let’s level up with technique and flow.

Bring your board and suitable gear. Weekly schedule subject to tides and weather. Times may vary — check back often.

Advanced Concepts

Tailored coaching for advanced techniques and tricks.

Gear required. Focus on precision, timing, and terrain. Sessions move locations based on tide and conditions.
